Computer Support Specialist

Here at PC Whisperers we aid in elevating and expanding the qualities of PCs using our capable staff to program codes and extensions that we sell to customers. The job of a Computer Support Specialist will be to talk to customers who are having technological issues or computer interface concerns. You should be able to conversate with the customer to discern the necessary information regarding their concerns. With your abundance of knowledge in computer science, you should be able to analyse the issue quickly. Moreover, you should be able to calmly help the customer navigate the best possible solutions that may resolve their distress.
